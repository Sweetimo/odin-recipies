Breakfast Burrito

Description

An excelent way to start any day. Sausages and eggs wrapped in a handy and easy to eat package. This take on the Mexican classic is a must try!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cups hash brown potatoes
  • 8 eggs
  • 1 can chopped green chillies
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 6 cooked sausages, cut into pieces
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shreaded cheese

Directions

  1. In a pan, add the oil over medium high heat. Add hash brown potatoes, cook for 7 minutes, turn over, and cook for 5 more minutes. Remove from pan.
  2. Beat eggs, and mix in chillies, salt and pepper. Mix until well incorporated. Cook sausage for 2 minutes untill brown, add the egg mixture, stiring throughout until scrambled
  3. assemble the burritoes, Spoon hash brown into the center of the flower tortillas, add sausage and ege mixture on top. Top with cheese. Do not over fill.
  4. Roll each burritos securly. Ready to enjoy now, or freeze for later.
