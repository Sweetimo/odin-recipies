Oyakodon

Description

Chicken and eggs prepared in a flavourful broth, served on rice. The ultimate comfort food!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oild
  • 1 cup Hondashi
  • 1 chicken breast, cut into small pieces
  • 1 onion, cut into slices
  • 2 eggs
  • scalions, sliced small for topping
  • 2 cups prepared rice

Directions

  1. Beat the eggs until well mixed
  2. Add oil to pan, fry the onions and chicken untill begining to brown
  3. Add the dashi to the pot and bring to a boil, turn down heat and let simmer for 1 minute.
  4. Add the egg mixture to the pan, cover and turn off the heat, allow the residual heat to cook the eggs
  5. Pour the contents of the pan over a bowl of freshly prepared rice, top with scallions to taste. Enjoy!
