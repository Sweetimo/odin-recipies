Okonomiyaki

Description

A classic from Kansai japan, sometimes called Japanese pizza. This cabbage filled pancake is delicious and filling and fun to have with friends!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 can chopped green chillies
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/3 cup japanese dashi
  • 1 head cabbage finely sliced
  • 1/2 lb sliced pork belly
  • Okonomiyaki sauce
  • Mayonaise
  • Katsuoboshi

Directions

  1. Mix flour, eggs, salt, sugar, cabage and dashi into a large mixing bowl.
  2. In a pan, heat oil to medium high, add sliced pork belly and cook until slightly done.
  3. Pour mixture over the sliced pork belly and cover the pan, leave for 3 minutes, or until bubbles form on the top of the mixture. Flip and leave for 3 more minutes.
  4. Remove from pan, add okonomiyaki sauce, mayonaise and Katsuoboshi on top, in that order. Best eaten hot!
